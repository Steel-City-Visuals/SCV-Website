Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Steel City Visuals LLC

Steel City Visuals LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit our contact form — name, email address, phone number, and message content.
  • Book a service — booking details collected through our third-party portal (portal.steelcityvisuals.com).

We do not collect any information automatically (e.g., cookies, analytics) through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or service request.
  • Coordinate and fulfill booked services.
  • Send follow-up communications directly related to your project.

We do not use your information for marketing without your consent, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Submissions are transmitted to Formspree's servers before being forwarded to our email. View Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. View Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — this site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect limited server log data. View GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and project-related correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill the services requested and comply with any legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent for any future communications.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at team@steelcityvisuals.com.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
